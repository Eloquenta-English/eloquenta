◆ Part 2 — Long Turn (3-4 min)

Use all the prompts on the card. Structure your answer: past &rarr; present &rarr; future. Don't memorise scripted answers.

◆ Part 3 — Discussion (4-5 min)

Give balanced opinions. Acknowledge both sides before giving your own view. Use phrases like "On one hand... on the other hand..."
